Abhishek Nandakumar

A small group of friends, family, or roommates who want a fair and predictable way to share a car.

In for business expenses on the road and personal use when at home.The system aims to immerse interactors in an environment through the tangible key object to perform tasks like reserving a car, performing scheduling activity or going for a quick ride.

Amrutha Krishnan

Design an interactive system to facilitate sharing so that users can easily plan trips, see the status and availability of the car, and track and control individual and shared use.

Looking ahead, think about a world where energy is more scarce, and car ownership is more expensive. Imagine that we need a way to support widespread access to cars, but still limit the overall number of cars in the world and on the roads.

Andy Pruett

Support use of keys and car in a way that is natural and familiar to users. Extend the Driver:Key:Car relation of ownership.
